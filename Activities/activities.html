Everybody Eats

Introduces youth to Indoor Agriculture. The first pilot session will be a collaboration with an area elementary school that will introduce youth to the process of aquaponic and hydroponic growing of produce.

We serve the community of Madison, Wisconsin through encouraging, empowering and supporting the needs of challenge deprived (at risk) youth who lack an abundance of healthy support systems, guidance, and encouragement.

Cooking for the Culture
