You Can Make a Difference

Investing in our youth creates a stronger community. We believe that when youth have strong mentors and leaders in their life it builds long lasting change, not only for their childhood, but for them as they grow and become adults. Your donation allows for us to make our youth that much closer to building stronger men.


We need to revive some of our old spaces to create new spaces. The main thing is asking the youth what they want and what they feel would be beneficial for them. Bringing them to the table and then acting on that. We can never help or empower them without bringing them to a place of empowerment.
